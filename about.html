About Us:

Papa Louie’s Pizza Parlor was founded in 1970. With now over 50 years’ experience, we can say without a doubt that we have the greatest pizza in Michigan. We currently own 3 different locations across the state. Our pizza parlor was originally family owned and operated. As of now, we are looking into expanding our locations to all across the state!
